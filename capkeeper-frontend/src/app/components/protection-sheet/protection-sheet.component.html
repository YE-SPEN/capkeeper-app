How it works...

Select your keepers from the sheet below. A complete protection sheet must include a minimum of 5 forwards, 3 defensemen, and 1 goalie plus up to 6 bench spots of any position. The main sheet has a cap limit of $45M, while the bench has a cap limit of $5M plus half of the unused space from the main sheet. You may designate one franchise player to exclude their cap hit from the main sheet limit. However, franchise players will still count toward the positional limits on the main sheet.
